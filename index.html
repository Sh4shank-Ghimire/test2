Neem Tree (Azadirachta indica)

Attribute Details
1. Common Name Neem Tree
2. Local Name (in Nepali) निमको रुख (Nimko Rukh)
3. Botanical Name Azadirachta indica
4. Family Mahogany family (Meliaceae)
5. Origin Native to India, Bangladesh, Pakistan, and other areas of the Indian subcontinent
6. Climate Tropical and subtropical climates
7. Tree Type Evergreen tree
8. Environmental Benefit Helps in purifying air, preventing soil erosion, and serves as a natural pest repellent
9. Human Uses Used for medicinal purposes, in cosmetics, as a pesticide, and in agricultural practices
10. Cultural Significance Revered in Hindu culture, often planted near homes for its medicinal properties and spiritual benefits
11. Toxicity Generally non-toxic but can be harmful in large quantities, especially the seeds and leaves
12. Lifespan Up to 200 years
13. Growth Rate Fast-growing tree, can grow up to 3-5 feet per year
14. Conservation Status Least Concern (LC) - Not endangered
15. Protection Status No special protection status required, widely cultivated
